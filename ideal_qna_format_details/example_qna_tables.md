# Example: Q&A with Tables
## Finance
### Expense Reimbursement

**Q: What are the daily per-diem rates for travel?**
**A:** The rates vary by location as shown in the table below:

| Location | Rate (USD) | Includes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tier 1 City | $120 | Food + Incidentals |
| Tier 2 City | $90 | Food + Incidentals |
| International | $150 | Food + Incidentals |

Note: Receipts are not required for per-diem claims, but travel logs must be submitted.
