# Example: Atomic Q&A (Standard Format)
## HR Policy
### Remote Work

**Q: What is the remote work policy?**
**A:** Our company follows a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office Tuesday through Thursday, with Monday and Friday being optional remote days. Exceptions can be made by department heads for certain roles.

**Q: Are there any equipment stipends?**
**A:** Yes, employees can claim up to $500 for home office setup expenses once every two years. This includes monitors, chairs, and desks.
