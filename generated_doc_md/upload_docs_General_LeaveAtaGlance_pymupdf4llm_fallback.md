|**5.**|_Grant of Leave on Medical Grounds:_<br>_a) An Employee who is a CHSS beneficiary and residing within the limits of Institute at_<br>_the time of illness should produce medical certificate/ fitness certificate from our_<br>_panel doctor._<br>_b) In respect of employee who has opted out of CHSS facilities and availing the medical_<br>_facilities provided by his/ her spouse’s employer, the medical practitioners approved_<br>_by that employer is allowed._<br> <br>_c) Where CHSS beneficiary proceeds outside Headquarter on duty, leave, etc., he should_<br>_produce the certificate from CGHS hospital/ AMA of Central Government/State_<br>_Government (not below the rank of an assistant surgeon grade I (Medical Graduate),_<br>_or other medical officer appointed by the Government to attend its officers in the_<br>_station)._<br> <br>_d) Where an  employee (Non-Gazetted) finds it difficult to obtain Medical_<br>_certificate/fitness certificate from CGHS/AMA within a radius of 8 km from his_<br>_residence or place of temporary stay outside his headquarters , the leave sanctioning_<br>_authority may consider grant of leave on the basis of the certificate of an RMP after_<br>_taking into account the circumstances of the case._<br> <br>_e) Second Medical Opinion:- The sanctioning authority has discretion to obtain second_<br>_medical opinion by addressing the Civil Surgeon/ Authorised AMO under intimation to_<br>_the official concerned._<br> <br>_f) Fitness Certificate: -The employee who has been granted leave on Medical Certificate_<br>_will be allowed to join duty only on production of fitness certificate in the prescribed_<br>_form/format. He will be allowed to join only from the date of fitness as certified or_<br>_thereafter, but not before. If he joins after the date of fitness, the intervening period_<br>_will be treated as leave without Medical Certificate._<br> <br>_g) Prefixing/ Suffixing holidays:- Holidays falling on the date of Medical Certificate (MC)_<br>_or on the following days will be treated as part of leave. But holidays preceding the_<br>_date of MC can be prefixed. In case of fitness certificate, holidays preceding the date_<br>_of fitness certificate will be treated as part of leave while those falling on the date of_<br>_fitness certificate and thereafter can be suffixed. (For example, if the MC is issued on_<br>_11th for leave from 11th to 20th with holidays on 9,10,21,22, all the four holidays can_<br>_be availed, provided the fitness certificate is issued on 21st. If the MC is issued on 10th _<br>_for leave from 11th, holiday on the 10th will form part of the leave.  Similarly, if the_<br>_fitness certificate is issued on 23rd, Holidays on 21st and 22nd will be part of leave._|


|6. Overstayal of leave: Any absence after the expiry of leave will be treated as overstayal of<br>leave unless it is regularized by grant of leave due. Period of such overstayal will be debited to<br>HPL at credit and any period in excess of HPL at credit will be treated as Extra Ordinary Leave.<br>No leave salary is admissible for the period of overstayal.|Col2|
